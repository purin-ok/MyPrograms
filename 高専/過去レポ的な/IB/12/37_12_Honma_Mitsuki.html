3$G(s)$, 1, $u(t) = 1$$\delta x(t)$$x_\infty$$K_P$, $K_D$. $L[\delta x(t)] = X(s) = G(s)U(s)$

\begin{eqnarray} x_\infty &=& \lim_{t \to \infty}\delta x(t) \nonumber\\ &=& \lim_{s \to 0}sX(s) \nonumber\\ &=& \lim_{s \to 0}sG(s)U(s) \nonumber\\ &=& \lim_{s \to 0}G(s) \nonumber\\ &=& \frac{3K_P}{3K_P - 1500} \nonumber\\ &=& \frac{K_P}{K_P - 500} > 1 \nonumber \end{eqnarray}
任意課題：式(1)の微分方程式から式(2)の伝達関数を導出せよ．¶

$X(s) = \mathcal{ L }[\delta x(t)]$$V(s) = \mathcal{ L }[\delta v(t)]$$I(s) = \mathcal{ L }[\delta i(t)]$(1)

\begin{eqnarray} M\{s^2X(s)-s\delta x(0) - \delta' x(0)\} &=& K_xX(s) + K_iI(s)\nonumber\\ L_m\{sI(s) - \delta i(0)\} + R_mI(s) &=& V(s)\nonumber \end{eqnarray}

$x(0) = x_0i(0) = i_0v(0) = v_0$$\delta x(0) = \delta' x(0) = \delta i(0) = 0$

\begin{eqnarray} Ms^2X(s) &=& K_xX(s) + K_iI(s)\nonumber\\ L_msI(s) + R_mI(s) &=& V(s)\nonumber \end{eqnarray}

$X(s)V(s)$

\begin{eqnarray} X(s) &=& \frac{K_iI(s)}{Ms^2 - K_x}\nonumber\\ V(s) &=& (L_ms + R_m)I(s)\nonumber \end{eqnarray}

$P(s) = X(s)\ /\ V(s)$

\begin{eqnarray} P(s) = X(s)\ / \ V(s) = \frac{K_i}{(Ms^2 - K_x)(L_ms + R_m)}\nonumber \end{eqnarray}
